The Only Soccer Game I Have Been To

Fortunately, I got to go to one soccer game in my life and it happened to be here in my home city of Saint Louis. Real Madrid came to play Inter Milan at what at the time was the Edward Jones Dome. It was the most amazing experience that I have ever had at any sporting event. Except for most recently the Blues at the Stanley Cup.
